# Koi Software - Project Context & Brand Guidelines

## 🏢 About the Company
**Company Name:** Koi Software
**Core Philosophy:** "We are building for our people."

## 🎯 Target Audience
Local businesses and entrepreneurs who need dedicated, accessible software solutions to scale and operate efficiently.

## 🛠️ Products & Offerings
**1. Individual SaaS Applications** (Will have separate landing pages):
- **Spa Administration Software:** Management tools for spa operations.
- **Nutritionist Dashboards:** Platforms for dietitians to manage patient data and plans.
- **Restaurant Web Pages:** Digital presence and management for the food industry.

**2. Automatizations & Systems** (Included on the main page):
- **Chatbots:** Intelligent 24/7 customer service agents.
- **Funnels & CRM:** Streamlining sales and managing client relationships.
- **Channel Automatizations:** Connecting WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger for unified communication.

**3. Consulting & Custom Solutions:**
- **Software Accessories & Add-ons:** Custom features for existing infrastructure.
- **System Optimization:** Improving current technical workflows.
- **Tailored Software:** Bespoke solutions built from the ground up to fit unique business needs.

## 💡 Value Proposition (The "Why")
- **Information Management:** Helping businesses organize and manage their data effectively.
- **Service Enhancement:** Enabling local businesses to provide superior service through technology.
- **Efficiency:** Saving business owners time and money.
- **Growth:** Boosting sales and operational capacity.
- **Visibility:** Enhancing brand visibility and client interaction through social media and digital channels.

## 🎨 Design Vision (Landing Page Redesign)
**Overall Vibe:** The *High-End Studio* look combined with the *Warm Partner* messaging.
- **Inspiration:** *Peek Insights* (sleek, conversion-focused structure, polished execution).
- **Visual Style:** Premium, high-contrast, modern aesthetics. Deep backgrounds with striking Koi red/orange accents, crisp typography, and "bento box" style layouts.
- **Micro-interactions & Motion:** Apple-level polish. Thoughtful hover animations, fluid transitions, and parallax scrolling to create an immersive experience.
- **Messaging Focus:** Approchable and human-centric. "Tech built locally, for local businesses."

## 🏗️ Landing Page Structure (The Blueprint)
The overall flow will closely follow the highly effective structure of Peek Insights.

1. **Hero Section:**
   - A bold, high-contrast headline.
   - An engaging subheadline focusing on efficiency and growth.
   - A clear Call-to-Action (CTA).

2. **Social Proof (Logo Carousel):**
   - An infinite-scroll carousel of client and partner logos to build immediate trust, just like Peek Insights.

3. **SaaS Offerings (Bento Box Grid):**
   - Deep dive into your specific products (Spa, Nutrition, Restaurants).
   - High-quality visual cards.
   - **Note:** These cards will link out to individual landing pages (to be built later).

4. **Automatizations Section:**
   - A cohesive section explaining how you unify communications and automate sales.
   - Covers Chatbots, Funnels, CRM, and all Channel Automatizations.
   - Does not link away; explains the value directly on the page.

5. **"Building for Our People" (The 'Why'):**
   - The core philosophy front and center. Emphasizing that you are the technical partner local businesses need to scale without stress.

6. **Consulting & System Optimization:**
   - Highlighting your services beyond standard SaaS: Software accessories, workflow consulting, and optimizing existing systems.

7. **Tailored Software & Final CTA:**
   - A grand finale Call-to-Action for bespoke projects and Custom Software.
   - Primary instruction: Contact `info@koisoftware.com` to start building their custom solution.

8. **Footer:**
   - Standard links, contact details, and social channels.
